Appraisal Services

We offer comprehensive appraisal services to determine the quality and value of your raw materials. Our expert appraisers use industry-standard methods and technologies to provide accurate and reliable assessments.

  • Visual inspection and documentation
  • Chemical analysis and testing
  • Microscopic examination
  • Comparison with industry standards
  • Detailed appraisal reports

Service Fees

Our service fees are based on a transparent pricing structure, typically calculated per kilogram of raw material appraised. The exact fee may vary depending on the complexity of the appraisal and the specific requirements of the client.
