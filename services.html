Our Services

Connecting manufacturers and laboratories globally for seamless raw material trade.

Commission Agent

We act as your trusted intermediary, connecting you with reliable manufacturers and laboratories worldwide. Our expertise in market dynamics and negotiation ensures you get the best deals and seamless transactions.

  • Source and qualify potential partners
  • Negotiate favorable terms and conditions
  • Facilitate communication and build trust
  • Ensure smooth and transparent transactions

POL/POD Handling

We manage the entire shipping process from the Port of Loading (POL) to the Port of Delivery (POD), ensuring efficient and timely delivery of your raw materials. Our services include:

  • Coordination with shipping lines and freight forwarders
  • Documentation and customs clearance
  • Cargo tracking and updates
  • Safe and secure handling of your goods

Customs Brokerage

Navigating complex customs regulations can be challenging. Our expert customs brokers ensure your goods clear customs efficiently and compliantly. We handle:

  • Tariff classification and duty calculation
  • Preparation and submission of customs documents
  • Compliance with import/export regulations
  • Minimize delays and potential penalties

Shipping

We offer comprehensive shipping solutions tailored to your specific needs. Our services include:

  • Selection of optimal shipping routes and modes
  • Negotiation of competitive freight rates
  • Cargo insurance and risk management
  • Door-to-door delivery services

Consignee/Notify Party

We can act as your designated consignee or notify party, ensuring smooth and reliable delivery of your goods. Our services include:

  • Receipt and verification of goods
  • Communication with shippers and customs officials
  • Arrangement of local transportation and delivery
  • Secure warehousing and storage solutions

Local Transportation

We provide efficient and reliable local transportation services to ensure your goods reach their final destination safely and on time. Our services include:

  • Trucking and delivery services
  • Route optimization and tracking
  • Handling of customs documentation
  • Secure and timely delivery to your doorstep
